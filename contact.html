Contact Form

Need help? We have many ways that you can contact us, based on the urgency of the situation. For general requests, please use the form below.










Urgency

Need other ways to contact us? Use the guide below to see how urgently you need your problem to be solved.

Minor

Services needed within the year? Write a letter to one of our hometowns or come find one of us in one of our hometowns that is closest to you. If you ask around, you will be sure to find us or know where we are headed.








Major

Services needed within the month? Come find us in one of our main cities and contact one of our allies to find us.





Critical

Services needed within the week? Use a Sending spell to contact one of us, and we can come to you by the end of the week. Please note that you can only use this option if you have ever contacted us before. If you cannot cast spells, you can purchase a Sending spell scroll for about 800 gold pieces.
