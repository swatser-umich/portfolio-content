Contact Form

Need help? We have many ways that you can contact us, based on the urgency of the situation. For general requests, please use the form below.

Need other ways to contact us? Use the guide to see how urgently you need your problem to be solved.

Minor

Services needed within the month? Write a letter to one of our hometowns or come find one of us in one of our hometowns that is closest to you. If you ask around, you will be sure to find us or know where we are headed.








Major

Services needed within the week? Write a letter to one of our common cities that we travel to regularly. If you ask around, you will be sure to find us or know where we are headed. You can also contact our allies in these cities.





Critical

Services needed within the day? Use a Sending spell to contact one of us, and we can come to you by the end of the week. Please note that you can only use this option if you have ever contacted us before. If you cannot cast spells, you can purchase a Sending spell scroll for about 800 gold pieces.
